Spicy Salmon Sushi Baked


Ingredients

Sushi Rice

  • 1-1.5 lbs salmon fillet
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 tbsp spicy mayo
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese

Homemade Spicy Mayo

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/3 cup sriracha
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Toppings

  • Eel sauce or other sweet sauce like unagi sauce
  • Furikake
  • Sesame seeds
  • Scallions, chopped
  • Sliced Jalapenos

Preparations

  1. Preheat the oven to 375F.
  2. Rinse the sushi rice then add it to a pot with the water. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer. Cook for 15-20 minutes until all the water is absborbed.
  3. Remove from flame and searson with the rice vinegar, salt, and sugar. Stir to combine then set aside.
  4. While the rice cooks, season the salmon with salt and pepper. Combine the coconut aminos, honey and garlic in a bowl and rub over the fish.
  5. Bake the salmon for 15-18 minutes (depending on thickness)
  6. After the salmon is cooked, flake it with a fork (without the skin) and add it to a bowl with the scallions, cream cheese, and spicy mayo. Combine together and mix evenly.
  7. Change the oven temperature to 400F.
  8. Flatten the rice into a 9x9 baking dish, then sprinkle furikake seasoning over the top.
  9. Add the salmon layer with more furikake, then drizzle over some swee sauce and spicy mayo.
  10. Bake the dish for 10 minutes, then set it under the broiler for 2-3 minutes.
  11. Once done, top with more furikake, sesame seends, sauces, scallions and jalapenos.
  12. Enjoy!
