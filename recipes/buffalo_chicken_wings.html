Baked Buffalo Wings


Ingredients

Crispy Baked Wings

  • 4 lbs (2 kgs) chicken wings
  • 5 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt

Sauce

  • 4 tbsp (60 g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Blue Cheese Dip

  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove small garlic, minced
  • 1-3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Black pepper

Preparations

Crispy Baked Wings

  1. Lay the wings on a rack on a rimmed baking trayand let them dry uncovered in the fridge, overnight. Alternatively, pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Prehear the oven to 250F. Put one oven shelf in the lower quarter of the oven and one in the top quarter.
  3. Line the tray with foil, then sprak the rack on the baking tray with oil.
  4. Place the wings in a large bowl. Sprinkle over baking powder and salt. Use hands to toss well (or shake in ziplock bag) to coat wings evenly.
  5. Place the wings skin side up on the rack. Bake on the lower shelf in the oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Then move the tray up to the higher shelf, and turn the oven up to 425F. Bake for a further 40-50 minutes, rotating the tray halfway through. The wings do not need to be turned.
  7. The wings are ready when they are dark golden brown and the skin is very crispy.
  8. Toss the Hot Sauce and serve immediately with Blue Cheese Dip and celery sticks. They can be served plain, with a dipping sauce or tossed in sauce.

Sauce

  1. Whisk together the Sauce ingredients. Keep warm or rehear just prior to using.

Blue Cheese Dip

  1. Mash the blue cheese with sour cream until smooth (or to your taste)
  2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well until combined, using milk to get it to the consistency you want.
  3. Store in the fridge until required. Remove from the fridge 15 minutes beofre serving.
